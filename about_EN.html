About GenDot

All foreigners can become locals.

Vision

GenDot is dedicated to creating an exclusive community for international students, aiming to facilitate the exchange of diverse cultures, information, and experiences. We strive to foster a positive living experience for those living abroad, connecting them to a supportive social network, and making Taiwan their second home where dreams come true.
